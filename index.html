Welcome to My Website

I am a postdoctoral researcher in AI and Education in the School of Information at the University of Michigan. My research interests include large language models as a tool for educational research, simulated student (and teacher) modeling, optimal practice scheduling, and AI-assisted methods for analyzing learning interaction data.
